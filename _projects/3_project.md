## 项目概述

本项目涉及智能制造过程的仿真与优化，应用数字孪生概念来提高制造效率和质量控制。

### 主要贡献

- 应用优化算法改进工艺流程
- 参与智能制造系统设计

## Overview

This project involves simulation and optimization of intelligent manufacturing processes, applying digital twin concepts to improve manufacturing efficiency and quality control.

### Key Contributions

- Developed simulation models for manufacturing processes
- Applied optimization algorithms for process improvement
- Contributed to intelligent manufacturing system design
